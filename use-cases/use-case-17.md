# USE CASE: 15 produce a report on all capital cities in the world organised by largest population to smallest.

## CHARACTERISTIC INFORMATION

### Goal in Context

As an *Staff Member* I want *to produce a report on all capital cities in the world organised by largest population to smallest*

### Scope

Company.

### Level

Primary task.

### Preconditions

We know the role.  Database contains current world population data.

### Success End Condition

A report is available for a Staff Member to view all capital cities organised largest population to smallest.

### Failed End Condition

No report is produced.

### Primary Actor

Staff Member.

### Trigger

A request for all capital cities in the world organised largest population to smallest is sent to staff member.

## MAIN SUCCESS SCENARIO

1. Request for all capital cities in the world organised largest population to smallest is sent to Staff Member.
2. Staff Member extracts all capital cities in the world organised largest population to smallest.
3. Staff Member produces report.


## SUB-VARIATIONS

None.
